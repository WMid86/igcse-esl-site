IGCSE 0511 Report Writer

Plan, draft, and check your report — step by step

What makes a strong report?
  • Use formal language throughout
  • Include a clear title
  • Organise ideas under subheadings
  • Explain who requested the report
  • Explain why the report was written
  • Explain how information was collected
  • Present findings clearly, avoiding personal opinions
  • End with sensible conclusions or recommendations

1 Report Title

Example titles you could adapt:
  • Report on Healthy Eating Habits Among Teenagers
  • Report on the Use of Technology in the Classroom
  • Report on After-School Activities and Student Wellbeing
