Paper 2 Listening Strategies

This page offers advice for the listening component of the IGCSE ESL exam. Use these tips to improve your listening skills and better prepare for the examination.

Listening Tips

  • Listen widely and often: Spend time listening to different speakers and topics. The more listening you do, the better your comprehension will become【52116344114370†L1744-L1752】.
  • Practise listening for key words and phrases: Try to predict when a particular word or phrase may be said and focus your attention【52116344114370†L1815-L1826】.
  • Take notes as you listen: In the exam you will need to fill in gaps using notes. Practise making notes of the main points using single words or short phrases while you listen【52116344114370†L1828-L1834】.
  • Use varied audio sources: Listen to podcasts, news reports and conversations to familiarise yourself with different accents and contexts. Use past exam recordings or sample tasks when available.

Practice Idea

Find a short podcast or conversation online. Listen once to get the overall meaning. Listen again and jot down key points or fill in a notes table. Compare your notes with the original recording.
