Cambridge IGCSE ESL (0511) Exam Portal

This site hosts a growing collection of resources for the Cambridge IGCSE 0511 English as a Second Language course. Use the interactive panels below to explore materials aligned with the exam structure. New activities will be added over time.

Visitors must enter the shared username and password provided to you. Analytics are collected using GoatCounter (a privacy‑friendly, open‑source analytics tool).

Paper 1 – Reading & Writing

Two-hour paper combining reading comprehension and writing tasks. 60 marks (50 % of total grade).

  • Reading: comprehension and multiple-matching tasks (coming soon)
  • Writing:
    • Report Writing
    • Email Writing
    • Writing Assistant (Grammar & Style Checker)
    • Review/Article Writing (coming soon)

Paper 2 – Listening

Approximately 50-minute listening paper with various short and long audio texts. 40 marks (25 % of total grade).

  • Listening practice & resources (coming soon)

Paper 3 – Speaking

10–15 minute speaking test including an interview, short talk and discussion. 40 marks (25 % of total grade).
